Given an array arr[] of size N. The task is to find the sum of the 
contiguous subarray within a arr[] with the largest sum.

Examples:

Input: arr = {-2,-3,4,-1,2,1,-5,3}
Output: 7
Explanation: The subarray {4,-1, 2, 1, 5} has the largest sum 7.

Input: arr = {2}
Output: 2
Explanation: The subarray {2} has the largest sum 1.

Input: arr = {5,4,1,7,8}
Output: 23
Explanation: The subarray {5,4,1,7,8} has the largest sum 25.
